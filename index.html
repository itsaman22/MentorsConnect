Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mentor Connect? +

Mentor Connect is a platform that connects mentees with industry experts for guidance, skill development, and career advice through scheduled video sessions.

How can I book a session with a mentor? +

Simply sign up, browse through the list of available mentors, and use the integrated calendar to book a video session based on your availability.

Is the service free of charge? +

Mentor Connect offers both free and paid mentoring sessions depending on the mentor's availability and expertise.

Can I become a mentor? +

If you are an expert in your field and wish to share your knowledge, you can apply to become a mentor by filling out a simple application form on our website.
